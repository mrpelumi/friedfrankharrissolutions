    Our Diversity & Inclusion Council provides a forum for identifying, prioritizing and discussing diversity, equity and inclusion issues in alignment with Fried Frank's diversity and inclusion strategy.  In addition, the Council will develop and support related initiatives and programs benefitting our talent, clients and the community. This Council will comprise of partners, associates, and business services leaders and chaired by the Firm's Chairman.  The Council's mandate is to provide leadership to the Firm in recruiting, developing, promoting and retaining our diverse talent.  The Council will ensure the Firm's culture and work environment foster respect for diversity and a sense of inclusion, belonging and fairness throughout the Firm; and to embed our commitment to diversity, equity and inclusion into everyday behaviors, policies and processes for our people, clients and the community.
    David J. Greenwald Chairman
