    Working Parents Resource Group

      • Facebook
      • Twitter
      • LinkedIn
      • Digg
      • Google
    Fried Frank recognizes that balancing the demands of work with personal responsibilities can be challenging, especially for those who have, or are planning to have, children. The Firm's Working Parents Resource Group is a resource for parents, serving as a place for support. The group offers professional development and networking opportunities, fosters an ongoing dialogue around important issues relevant to group members, and enriches the professional and personal lives of individuals at Fried Frank.

    The following are examples of topics discussed at group meetings:
    • Child care
    • Parental leave
    • Alternative work arrangements
    • Balancing work and family
    • Schools and education
    • Eldercare
    • Financial and estate planning for families
    • Career development and advancement
