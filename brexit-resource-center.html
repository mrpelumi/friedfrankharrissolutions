    The United Kingdom's referendum to exit the European Union has resulted in economic and regulatory uncertainty for many organizations and individuals. Fried Frank's team of dedicated lawyers understands the relevant law and market conditions. We are here to share timely insight and clarity around the key developments to help you prepare for change, manage risks and embrace opportunities as Brexit developments unfold.

    This resource center connects you directly to:
    • Our team of lawyers focused on Brexit and its implications as they evolve
    • Our library of memos and alerts on Brexit-related topics
    • Our upcoming events and forums for ongoing dialogue about Brexit
